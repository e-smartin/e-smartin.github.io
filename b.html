Conclusions

  • Investment and Revenue aren't linearly correlated.
  • While the investment remains more or less the same, Revenue has an aggressive increasing tendency. Note that 2020 is affected by COVID, but after it, a growing tendency is observed.
  • The most profitable movie is Wolf Warrior 2, despite there being 81 movies with higher Revenues.
  • Most of the movie's revenue comes from outside the USA, which represents, on average, 35.02% of the total Revenue.
  • Walt Disney, Warner Bros, Universal Pictures, and Sony Pictures represent 80% of the total Revenues.
  • The first-week revenue seems to be a good indicator of the overall performance; 5 out of 10 movies that made it to the Top 10 in their first week ended up making the Total Top 10. Both Avatar movies weren't that popular the first week and ended up making the top 3.
